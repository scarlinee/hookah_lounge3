Nestled on Yathreb Street in Dabouq, Hookah Lounge is an international restaurant and café that weaves Arabic hospitality with a relaxed, modern warmth. Whether you come for breakfast, a long lunch, or a late-night shisha session — every detail is considered.

Open daily from 1 PM until 1 AM. Last orders taken at the restaurant. Follow us on Instagram at 16K followers and growing.
